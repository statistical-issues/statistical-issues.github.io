Welcome to Statistical Issues!

This a documentation web about statistical theory, modelling and implementation. We try to cover brief theory about models being used as well as implementations on some programming languages like R and Python.

Authors

  • Erick Chacon

Erick Chacon is a PhD student in Statistics and Epidemiology in the Faculty of Health and Medicine at Lancaster University, where he works in the research group CHICAS (Combining Health, Information, Computation and Statistics). Erick received a MSc. in Statistics from Lancaster University and a BSc. in Statistics from the National University of Engineering. He is passionate about spatial and spatio-temporal modelling, data visualization and computational methods.

  • Vilma Romero

Vilma Romero is a Lecturer in Statistics in the Statistical Engineering School at the National University of Engineering in Lima, Peru. Vilma received a MSc. in Statistics from Lancaster University and a BSc. in Statistics from the National University of Engineering. She loves learning and teaching R, with an emphasis on interactive data visualization and statistics.
